Project RogueRink

I'm Dylan, and I am the primary coder of RogueRink. I'm currently studying Coputer Science at CalState University Fullerton. Vist My Personal Site to learn more.

RogueRink abreviated R2 is an inovative project the focues on competition, evolution, and action.

RogueRink is composed on smaller projects that keep evolving. R2 is built on HTML 5, CSS, and JavaSript. The Website is built on BootStrap, while the games are built on top of Phaser or built by hand in JavaScript.

So far most of our games are small, but they will be expaned as new ideas are create. Thanks for visiting and hope to see you again!
